5.2 Perception of Prevention

Respondents were asked to, “Please indicate how much you agree or disagree with the following statements:” and were given the following response options:

  • Strongly agree
  • Agree
  • Neither agree nor disagree
  • Disagree
  • Strongly disagree

Respodents were specifically asked about the following types of prevention (including the overall benefit of preventive care):

  • Overall, preventive healthcare is beneficial.
  • Flu vaccines are beneficial to me.
  • Blood pressure screenings are beneficial for me.
  • Colonoscopies are beneficial for me.
  • Pap tests are beneficial for me.
  • Mammograms are beneficial for me.
  • Body Mass Index (BMI) screenings are beneficial for me.
  • Varicella (chicken pox/shingles) vaccines are beneficial for me.
  • Glaucoma screenings are beneficial for me.
  • Osteoporosis screenings are beneficial for me.
  • Pneumonia vaccines are beneficial for me.
  • Prostate screenings are beneficial for me.
